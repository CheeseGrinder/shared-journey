# Code of Conduct

This project's issue tracker and pull requests are a place to report bugs, discuss
features, and contribute code — not a place for harassment, personal attacks, or
discrimination of any kind.

In short:

- Be respectful. Disagree with ideas, not people.
- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks, on any basis.
- Keep discussions on topic and constructive.

Comments or conduct that don't meet this bar may be deleted, and repeat offenders may be
blocked from the repository, at the maintainer's discretion. This doesn't create any
obligation on the maintainer's side (see the [support policy](README.md)) — it just sets
the bar for how people are expected to treat each other here.
